Project overview

Our in-house animation team devised a project-specific character to integrate into our GAL offering, using programs such as Adobe Character Animator and Adobe Flash. The mixed, personalised approach features a hand-drawn ‘coach’ who provides an instant access point to learning content, acting as a host, connecting each section, and introducing the content. The character was voiced by a Senior Partner, recorded in our London studio and synched with the final product in post-production. This approach personalised the course from the start, and proved an imaginative way to convey the client’s message.

Project overview

With our ‘level 2’ animated character, with full mobility, the sky’s the limit. We will produce a fully-mobile character with a range of actions, gestures and facial expressions. One high-level example of this is our fully-animated video for BNPP AML. Our motion graphics animator, a natural digital storyteller, knew that having a distinctive, narrative concept would underpin the project’s theme and make the character’s journey more engaging and memorable. Working with our instructional designer, project manager, and two other motion graphics designers, this asset was successfully produced with an innovative and creative blend of storyboarding, illustration, character animation, sound design, video editing and compositing.
