Project overview

Our filming with bespoke animation, as used for our Oil and Gas videos, is a step beyond our standard head and shoulders offering. Employing our highly skilled animators as well as our filming team, the core content is filmed in our state-of-the-art green screen studio, cut and edited in house, and then enhanced with animated backgrounds. These animations can convey statistics, tables, related visuals and hand-drawn characters. The end result allows us to convey supplementary information alongside the central message conveyed by the speaker.
