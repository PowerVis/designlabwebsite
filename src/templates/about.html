Brandon Hall Awards

The Brandon Hall Group

Best Use of Video for Learning 2015

Best Use of Blended Learning 2015

The most prestigious awards programme in the industry, The Brandon Hall Group Excellence Awards have been operating for an impressive 22 years, rewarding excellence since 1994. Prizes are handed out over more than a hundred categories.

Organisations that have successfully deployed programs, strategies, modalities, processes, systems, and tools that have achieved measurable results are recognised by the Excellence Awards. The program attracts entrants from leading corporations around the world, as well as mid-market and smaller firms. Solution providers with innovative, cutting-edge technologies, products and services also have the opportunity to enter.

The Learning Awards

The Learning Performance Institute

Innovation in Learning Award Finalist 2016

Gold Accredited Learning Provider 2015

The flagship awards of The Learning Performance Institute celebrate outstanding examples of high standards, best practice, innovation and excellence in Learning and Development. Categories include Learning Provider of the Year, Learning Professional of the Year, the Learning Technologies Award and the Innovation in Learning Award. The Institute divides each category into Gold, Silver and Bronze awards. The Institute also provide accreditation for learning providers.

TJ Awards

Training Journal

Shortlisted, 2015

The prestigious learning publication Training Journal runs it annual awards ceremony every year as part of a gala dinner. 16 awards and a Special Achievement Award are announced on the night, with the occasion supported by over 500 sponsors. Awards include Best Use of Technology in Learning, L&D Team of the Year and L&D Professional of the Year.
