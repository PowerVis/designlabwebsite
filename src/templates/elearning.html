Project overview

Our templated option for eLearning is based around quick-to-deploy content population tools. Each section of the course is templated in advance, then populated from content devised by an Instructional Designer and Subject Matter Resource, or SMR, usually provided by the client. In the case of Anti Money-Laundering, or AML, much of the content was adapted and modified from elements of an existing course, core animations were used to strengthen learning points and a simple, accessible design ensured it was a high-impact, low-timescale piece of eLearning, perfectly aligned to EY’s brand identity.

Project overview

Coexist was a markedly different client for Design Lab – a non-profit organisation that seeks to promote religious literacy, awareness and tolerance in the workplace. This therefore was a very sensitive undertaking, requiring constant input from the client and a completely bespoke package.

Working closely with Coexist and our EY Senior Partner, we developed a comprehensive eLearning course illustrating the challenges surrounding religious literacy and tolerance in the workplace. Each page was heavily customised to match Coexist’s strong visual identity and brand guidelines.
