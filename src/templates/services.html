Learning

eLearning

We produce highly-engaging online learning courses, containing a variety of animations, knowledge checks and full-blown assessment sections. To align with cost and time constraints, we can create your eLearning to one of our standard templates, but we also offer bespoke eLearnings that can be customised to your every requirement.

VILTs

Our VILTs (Virtual Instructor-Led Training) are eLearnings in which learners are guided through the session by a real person, in real time. As well as creating the course itself, we will write Facilitator Guides for the instructor to refer to throughout the session.

Classroom learning

We may be a Design Lab, but we haven't forgotten about traditional classroom learning. Our team will write and design print handouts, large-scale visual aids and digital resentations to suit the needs of both learner and facilitator.

Audio Visual Production

Animations & Videobytes

With a motley crew of animators, illustrators, digital artists and graphic designers to rival Pixar, our design team can create anything from simple animated logos to complex motion graphics and narrated, animated videos. We’ve also been known to spice up our output with some flashes of CGI and special effects. Both our animations and videobytes come in standard and advanced, according to the level of content you require.

Filming and Voiceover

Whether it’s a quick sketch, interview, voiceover or fully-narrated, informational ‘video byte’, our video production specialist is on hand to make movie magic. Our studio boasts state-of-the-art cameras, lighting and audio recording equipment – including a separate recording booth. We’ve even got a clapper board.

Development and testing

Development

Our Development team is Design Lab’s last stop before bringing out eLearnings to fruition. But using their industry-leading knowledge and technological intuition, they also build web portals, SharePoint pages and fully-fledged, interactive websites to support your learning experience.

Testing

Every element of our digital platform products is rigorously tested by our Offshore QA team, ensuring both precision and quality for our clients.
